The Academic Success Team

The Academic Success Team is a sector within the Academic Programs and Student Life (APSL) team at UMSI. Our focus is to help students navigate personal and academic challenges, continuously broaden our resources to support UMSI's growing student body, and provide students with peer tutors and advisors.
