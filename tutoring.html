Tutoring Options at UMSI

Achieving academic success often requires additional support outside of the classroom. At the University of Michigan's School of Information, we are committed to providing you with the resources you need to excel. Explore the tutoring options available to our Bachelor of Science in Information (BSI) students.

Peer Tutoring Program

Our Peer Tutoring Program connects you with fellow students who have excelled in UMSI courses and are eager to share their knowledge and study strategies. Our peer tutors offer:

  • One-on-one or group tutoring sessions
  • Help with understanding difficult conceptss
  • Assistance with specific coursework in core BSI classes

For more information, please visit our UMSI Tutoring page.

University-wide Tutoring Services

There are also tutoring options open to all university students. Below you will find a list of a few of the university's other resources:

  • Mathematics Learning Center (MLC):
    Support for all levels of mathematics, useful for quantitative courses within the BSI curriculum
  • Science Learning Center (SLC):
    Tutoring for chemistry, biology, and physics courses, which can complement the technical skillsets of BSI students.
  • Sweetland Center for Writing:>
    Improve your writing skills for papers, reports, and projects with writing workshops and one-on-one consultations.
