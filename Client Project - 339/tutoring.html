Peer Tutoring Program

Our Peer Tutoring Program connects you with fellow students who have excelled in UMSI courses and are eager to share their knowledge and study strategies. Our peer tutors offer one-on-one or group tutoring sessions, and assistance with specific coursework in core BSI classes

For more information, please visit our UMSI Tutoring page.

University-wide Tutoring Services

Aside from UMSI-specific tutoring opportunities, the University of Michigan offers many other tutoring services for all students! Below are a few of the services available to you.

Mathematics Learning Center (MLC): Support for all levels of mathematics, useful for quantitative courses within the BSI curriculum.

Science Learning Center (SLC): Tutoring for chemistry, biology, and physics courses, which can complement the technical skill sets of BSI students.

Sweetland Center for Writing: Improve your writing skills for papers, reports, and projects with writing workshops and one-on-one consultations.
