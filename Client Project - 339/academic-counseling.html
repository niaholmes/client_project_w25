Academic Advising

At UMSI, we have an Academic Advising Team who is committed to supporting our students' academic success by offering personalized guidance with navigating your curriculum, selecting courses, and addressing any academic challenges or questions you may encounter during your studies.

To schedule an appointment, visit our BSI Academic Advising Resource Site.

Peer Advising

The Academic Success Team put together a group of amazing graduate student interns, who provide peer tutoring and peer advising!

Learn more about peer advising opportunities through the Academic Programs and Student Life (APSL) team.

Career Development Support

The U-M School of Information offers extensive career development support tailored to BSI students, including resume and cover letter workshops, internship and job search strategies, and interview preparation!

Visit our Career Development page for more information.
