UMSI Student Care

If you or anyone you know needs support for a non-urgent concern, including but not limited to, academic difficulty, mental health, illness, financial hardship, extended absence or concerning behavior, please submit the UMSI Student Care Referral and our Academic Success Team will follow up within two business days.

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

At the University of Michigan, CAPS is the primary mental health service for U-M students, providing a range of confidential services designed to support your emotional and psychological well-being during your time at university. We are committed to fostering a safe and inclusive environment for all students. View their contact info below.
